So where do I go from here?

You can create snippets of code to share with others.

Make inline notes to suggest minor changes
or annotate major issues in the margins.

For general comments, a discussion section is available
at the bottom of the page for each snippet.

It's your code. You control who sees it.

Set your snippet to private to keep it limited with the people you share it with.
Public snippets can be seen by anyone.

Who is CodeBoard for?

CodeBoard was made in mind for education.

Students of all ages are free to work together on code
using CodeBoard as their medium.

However, CodeBoard can easily be scaled up
to act as an internal system for company employees.

CodeBoard can even be your company's solution for interviewing candidates for technical positions.

CodeBoard is open-source.

And we intend to keep it that way.
